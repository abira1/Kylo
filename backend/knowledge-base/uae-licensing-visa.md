# UAE Business Licensing & Visa Knowledge Base

## Free Zones & Mainland Licensing

**Free Zone**: A UAE Free Zone is a government-designated business jurisdiction that enables foreign investors to establish and own a company with up to 100% ownership. Each Free Zone is regulated by its own authority and may cater to specific industries.

**Mainland Company**: A company licensed by the relevant DED and permitted to operate throughout the UAE.

**DED (Department of Economy and Tourism)**: The government authority responsible for issuing mainland business licenses in each emirate.

**Trade License**: The official permit authorizing a business to conduct approved activities in the UAE. Must be valid for at least 30 days for visa applications.

## Visa Requirements

### Prerequisites for Visa Applications
- **Business License**: Must be valid for at least 30 days
- **Immigration Card (Establishment Card)**: Must be valid for at least 30 days
- **Visa Allocation**: Must be valid for at least 30 days

### Two Types of Visas

**Employee Visa**:
- Age: 18-63 years old
- Requires: Photo, Passport Copy, Degree Certificate (if required by designation)
- Bank Statement: Mandatory for Nigerian and Cameroon nationals only
- Professional Certification: Required for Pilots, Teachers, Medical and Legal professionals

**Investor/Partner Visa**:
- Age: 18+ years old
- Requires: Photo, Passport Copy, Bank Statement (mandatory for all nationalities)
- Minimum Share Capital: AED 50,000 or above shareholding required
- Additional Documents: Company Share Register, Business License, Lease Agreement, MOA

### Document Requirements

**Photo**:
- Passport size (35mm x 45mm)
- White background, no shadows
- Taken within 6 months
- JPG/JPEG/PNG format, max 2MB
- No glasses, no accessories
- Professional studio photo only (no Photoshop/AI generated)

**Passport Copy**:
- Minimum 180 days validity
- First (bio-data) page and external cover (colored copy)
- Normal passport only (no diplomatic/special passports)

## Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO)

**Definition**: A natural person who ultimately owns or controls a company (directly or indirectly) with 25% or more ownership.

**UBO Criteria - A natural person is a UBO if they**:
1. Own or control 25%+ of shares or voting rights
2. Ultimately own or control 25%+ of shares or voting rights (directly/indirectly)
3. Hold the right to appoint/remove majority of board of directors
4. Exercise significant influence or control over the company
5. Exercise ultimate control over management

**Required UBO Information**:
- Full name and aliases
- Place and date of birth
- Nationality and other citizenships
- Current residential address
- Date became beneficial owner
- Total shares/equity held

## Immigration & Establishment Card

**Establishment Card**: The company's registration with UAE immigration authorities. Enables the company to sponsor visas for investors, partners, employees, and dependents.

**Issued By**:
- **Mainland**: Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs and Port Security (ICP) or GDRFA in Dubai
- **Free Zone**: Submitted through relevant Free Zone Authority, coordinated with ICP/GDRFA

**Cannot sponsor visas without Establishment Card**:
- Cannot apply for employment entry permits
- Cannot apply for residence visas
- Cannot process visa issuance, renewals, or cancellations

**Renewal**: Generally renewed with trade license renewal cycle

**Amendments**: For trade name changes, ownership transfers, shareholder additions/removals, authorized signatory changes

## Key Compliance Areas

**KYC (Know Your Customer)**: Due diligence procedures to verify client identity and assess risks.

**AML (Anti-Money Laundering)**: Regulations to prevent concealment of illegally obtained funds.

**Source of Funds (SOF)**: Origin of money used for specific transactions/investments.

**Source of Wealth (SOW)**: Overall origin of accumulated wealth.

**DNFBP (Designated Non-Financial Business or Profession)**: Businesses subject to AML regulations - includes real estate brokers, precious metals dealers, corporate service providers.

## Common Designations & Requirements

- **Driver**: Must have valid UAE Driving License and Vehicle License
- **Pilot**: Attested pilot license from UAE MOFA + NOC from Ministry of Civil Aviation
- **Teacher**: Approval from KHDA if requested by immigration
- **Medical Professional**: License to practice from UAE Health Authority
- **Legal Professional**: NOC to practice from UAE courts

## UAE Pass & Tax

**UAE Pass**: UAE's national digital identity platform for accessing government services.

**VAT (Value Added Tax)**: Consumption tax applied to taxable goods and services in UAE.

**Tax Registration Number (TRN)**: Unique number issued by UAE tax authority for VAT or tax purposes.

## Immigration Abbreviations

- **GDRFA**: General Directitude of Residency and Foreigners Affairs
- **ICP**: Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs and Port Security
- **MOA**: Memorandum of Association
- **NOC**: No Objection Certificate
- **AOA**: Articles of Association
- **PEP**: Politically Exposed Person
